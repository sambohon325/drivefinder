Cached renders

Every image here is reused across every user who matches that exact make/model/color/angle — that's the whole cost-saving point. Delete one if it looks wrong; it'll regenerate fresh the next time someone needs it. Filters are by make/model/color/type — year and trim aren't tracked per render, since the same render is meant to be reused across every year and trim of a given model.
